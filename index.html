CodeChef SRM’s Ideology

CodeChef SRM gives credence to growing and succeeding cooperatively.We are a group of
like-minded coders trying to put a dent in this world. Our undertaking is to proceed with
strength of will and educate the crowd about the state of the art in technology. The key
ambition behind this initiative is to spread the gravity of competitive programming around
our campus, assisting students to find their place in the sun.

Our Seniors worked endlessly to ensure that the members achieved their true potential. And
that's what we expect from our young chefs. All we want from our family is to be open to
learning new things. We don’t believe in restricting our members to their central domain.
Despite this, we push them to flourish out of their zone , giving them a platform to expand
and plump for the field they find intriguing to learn.

All the members striving hard to shove their boundaries are heartened and stirred up with
timely swags like CodeChef Laddus and many more to proceed with their work more
passionately and outshine tremendously.
